CONCEPTUAL ANALYSIS:
[HIPOTEZA] Antyelekcja is a meta-institutional performance that questions what it means to participate in political processes when the process itself is intentionally empty. The urn with fish that never arrived is a physical manifestation of the "absence as presence" concept.

[HIPOTEZA] The project connects to the political performance cycle: Copyright → Antyelekcja → Wiktoria Cukt → Testy na Cyborga → NATO Now! Each project evolves the concept of institutional performance.

[HIPOTEZA] The urn object is a physical manifestation of the "empty system" - a voting mechanism without voters, an election without candidates, a performance without audience participation.
